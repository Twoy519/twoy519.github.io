A Bit About Us

Anna and Troy first met in San Francisco in 2012. Anna was in a bath towel. Troy was homeless. They lived together in the friend zone for a year and then fell madly in love when they realized they could cut down on rent by sleeping in the same bedroom. Troy eventually got his sh*t together and asked Anna to marry him in April of 2016. Their kids are gonna be cute as hell.
